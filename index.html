How NearU Works

What is NearU?

NearU is a private app that helps Waterloo students meet people they naturally cross paths with on campus — in lectures, gyms, libraries, and study spots.

Is it safe?

Yes. Only verified Waterloo students can join. Your exact location is never shared — NearU simply detects repeated patterns and suggests connections when it feels natural.
